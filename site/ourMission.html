Exposing the truth everywhere, giving you the information they dont want you to have.

The situation in bulgaria has been dire for decades because people are systematically lied to and denied acess the information they require to make informed deicsions. Our goal for the future is to allow people ot make choices with motive, not be blindly led by politicians or corporations that wish to exploit them. Our goal is to reveal every possible secret and leave no stone unturned in our pursuit for the truth.
Allowing people to have the knowledge of the secrets that are being kept from them will, in the long term, lead to a better country overall, aswell as a more intelligent population.
Our reporters are all about truth. Every day they are met with new and uncomfortable situations, because of their dedication to what we do, we can build our work with the materials they provide.
